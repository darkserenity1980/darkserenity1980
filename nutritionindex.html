Here is the Nutrition Information about Bananas! You can also find the Nutritional Values of Bananas!

  • Total Calories: 105
  • Total Fat: 0 g
  • Carbohydrates: 27 g
    • Dietary Fiber: 3 g
    • Total Sugars: 14 g
  • Protein: 1 g
    • Calcium: 6 mg
  • Sodium: 1 mg
    • Iron: 1 mg

But what does this mean? This means that there are health benefits from Bananas! Some of these Health Benefits include:

  • Banana promotes heart health! It contains a lot of potassium that helps elevate the heart.
  • They also contain Vitamin B6 and Vitamin C.
  • They can help your body produce serotonin as well.
  • They support the growth of gut bacteria as they contain resistant starch and pectins.
